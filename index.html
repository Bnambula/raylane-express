Simple Process

How It Works

Five steps from search to seat — under 3 minutes on any device.

1
Search Your Route
Enter from, to, and date. Raylane Fleet appears first — our own vetted vehicles. Third-party operators ranked by rating then price.
2
Select Seat
Choose your seat on the interactive map. Seat is locked server-side (Redis) for 5 minutes — only you can book it while locked.
3
Enter Your Details
Name and phone number. Option to pay 20% now and the rest at departure (advance booking).
4
Pay via Mobile Money
MTN MoMo or Airtel Money prompt sent to your phone. Webhook-verified instantly. Booking confirmed within seconds.
5
Get Your E-Ticket
QR e-ticket sent via SMS. Scan at boarding. No app needed. Works on any phone.
